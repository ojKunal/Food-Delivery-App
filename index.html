Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our login hours are from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Please note that our website is accessible 24/7, but customer support is available during these hours.

What do I do if I want a refund?

If you're not satisfied with your order, you can request a refund within 24 hours of delivery. Please contact our customer support team, and they will assist you through the refund process.

How long will it take for food to arrive?

The delivery time depends on your location and the current workload. Generally, we strive to deliver orders within 30 to 45 minutes of confirmation. However, please note that during peak hours, delivery times may vary.

Does your restaurant offer both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options?

Yes, our restaurant offers a wide range of options, including both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes to cater to everyone's preferences.

What is the cost of food delivery?

The cost of food delivery varies depending on your location and order size. You can view the delivery charges at checkout before confirming your order.

Who is eligible for a pro membership?

Our pro membership is available to frequent customers who order from us regularly. Pro members enjoy exclusive discounts, priority delivery, and special offers.
